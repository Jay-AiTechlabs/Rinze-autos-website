## Design Philosophy: LUXURY MINIMALISM WITH MODERN EDGE

### Core Principles
1. **Premium Simplicity:** Clean, uncluttered layouts that emphasize quality over quantity
2. **Dark Elegance:** Dark backgrounds with strategic metallic/gold accents for luxury feel
3. **Strategic Whitespace:** Breathing room that conveys confidence and exclusivity
4. **Modern Typography:** Bold display fonts paired with refined body text

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background:** Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) - sophisticated, professional
- **Accent Color:** Gold/Amber (#d4af37) - luxury, premium positioning
- **Secondary Accent:** Slate blue (#3b4a5c) - trust, reliability
- **Text:** Off-white (#f5f5f5) on dark backgrounds
- **Highlights:** Bright white (#ffffff) for CTAs and emphasis

### Typography System
- **Display Font:** Playfair Display (elegant, luxury-oriented)
- **Heading Font:** Poppins Bold (modern, strong presence)
- **Body Font:** Inter (clean, readable, professional)

### Layout Paradigm
- **Hero Section:** Full-width luxury car imagery with minimal text overlay
- **Grid System:** Asymmetric layouts with 3-column grids for inventory
- **Sections:** Stacked full-width sections with alternating dark/light backgrounds
- **Navigation:** Sticky top nav with minimal items, gold accent on hover

### Signature Elements
1. **Gold Accent Lines:** Subtle horizontal dividers between sections
2. **Car Cards:** Minimalist cards with hover zoom effect, gold border on hover
3. **Metallic Badges:** "Featured," "New Arrival," "Financing Available" badges with gold background

### Animation & Interaction
- **Hover Effects:** Subtle scale (1.02x) and gold border glow on car cards
- **Transitions:** 300ms ease-out for all interactive elements
- **Page Transitions:** Fade-in on scroll for sections
- **CTA Buttons:** Gold background with white text, slight scale on hover

### Key Sections
1. **Header/Navigation:** Sticky, minimal, with logo and contact CTA
2. **Hero Banner:** Full-width luxury car image with headline
3. **Featured Vehicles:** 3-column grid of premium cars
4. **Why Choose Us:** Trust indicators (24/7, transparent, quality)
5. **Services:** Financing, swap deals, inspections, international imports
6. **Latest Arrivals:** Scrollable carousel of new inventory
7. **Contact Section:** CTA with phone, email, location, WhatsApp
8. **Footer:** Minimal footer with links and social media
